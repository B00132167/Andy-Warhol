The Factory Pop-Art

“You need to let the little things that would ordinarily bore you suddenly thrill you.”
- Andy Warhol

Andy Warhol has one of the most underappreciated and best legacies of almost any artist. To this day Andy Warhol is still regarded as a leading pioneer of the Pop-Art movement. With many of his art works being prime examples of Pop Art

During his time he was revered and very popular, being able to have not just popular works as an artist but also being liked as a person is an amazing accomplishment

Whilst it is common for many artists to have their work become much more valuable after they have passed away. The recent selling of his Marilyn portrait for 195 million, making it one of the most expensive pieces of art sold is astounding
