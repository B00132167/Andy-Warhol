The Factory Pop-Art

This website is dedicated to the famous Pop-Artist Andy Warhol. The pages each have a specific topic which relate to Andy Warhol and even some which cover Pop-Art itself. The goal of this website is to provide the visitor with detailed knowledge on Andy Warhol and to be able to learn about Warhol in an easy to digest manner. This website is specifically intended for anybody and everybody who is interested in learning about Andy Warhol, whether that person is young or old. A student or simply somebody interested in expanding their knowledge in a particular area. With that in mind I hope that whoever you are and for whatever reason you are here, that you enjoy this website and gain new knowledge. We hope that you find this website interesting and more importantly that you come away feeling that you have learnt something. The pursuit of knowledge is one of the most important and pure journeys there is and we hope that you experience great success
