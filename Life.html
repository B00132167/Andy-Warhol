The Factory Pop-Art

Andy Warhol was born on the 6th of August in 1928 in Pennsylvania. From an early age art was a childhood hobby for Warhol. Warhol pursued his passion for art and graduated from college with a Fine Arts Degree. Following this he moved to New York City and began a career. He eventually became a prosperous commercial artist.

On the 3rd of June 1968 Andy Warhol was shot. Whilst Warhol did survive this attack he spent weeks in hospital going through multiple injuries. Following this he had to wear a surgical corset for the remainder of his life. Despite this Warhol continued as an artist. Over 10 years later as a result of health issues he went to hospital. Only days after entering hospital Andy Warhol suffered further complications resulting in his passing on the 22nd of February 1987 aged 58 years old.
